1. CHUCHUM SOGUI, ANDARI SOGUI, AP KUBI SOGUI.
    Chuchum Sogui Montong Chumok Chirugui. Iop Andari Sogui, Wesanteul Makki, 
    Ap Kubi, Baro Montong Chumok Chirugui, Pal Kup Chiruigui. Grito.

2. KUN GAM CHUMOK 1
    Ap Dip Kubi KunGam Iop Chumok Chirugui, Ap Kubi Bande Montong Chumok  
    Chirugui, Ap Chagui (kihap). Dip Dip Kubi Sogui, Montong Son Nal Makki.

3. KUN GAM CHUMOK 2
    Ap Dip Kubi, KunGam Iop Chumok Chirugui, An Son Nal Chigo, Bakkat Son Nal     
    Chigui Ap Kubi Bande Montong Chumok Chigui, Ap Chagui (Kihap). Dip Dip Kubi 
    Sogui, Montong Son Nal Koduro Makki.

4. KUN GAM CHUMOK 3

    Montong Son Nal Koduro Makki.
